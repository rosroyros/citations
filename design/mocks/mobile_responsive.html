How to Cite a Journal Article in APA

Complete guide to citing journal articles in APA 7th edition format

📖 10 min read
🔄 Dec 2024

📋 Quick Reference

Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Article title. Journal Name, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/...

Basic Format

Journal articles are one of the most commonly cited sources. The key elements include:

  • Author names in Last, F. M. format
  • Publication year in parentheses
  • Article title in sentence case
  • Journal name in title case (italicized)
  • Volume number (italicized)
  • Issue number in parentheses
  • DOI as URL

Example

Smith, J. A., & Jones, M. B. (2020). The effects of social media on academic performance. Journal of Educational Psychology, 112(4), 789-805. https://doi.org/10.1037/edu0000123

Common Errors

Using "and" instead of "&"

❌ Wrong:

Smith, J. and Jones, M. (2020)...

✓ Correct:

Smith, J., & Jones, M. (2020)...

Italicizing issue number

❌ Wrong:

Journal Name, 15(2), 123-145.

✓ Correct:

Journal Name, 15(2), 123-145.

Validation Checklist

  • Authors formatted correctly
  • Ampersand (&) before last author
  • Year in parentheses
  • Article title in sentence case
  • Journal name italicized
  • Volume italicized
  • Issue in parentheses
  • DOI as URL
  • No period after DOI
