# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 5, 2025**

## Introduction

Citation Format Checker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our citation validation service.

By using Citation Format Checker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Citation Content**: When you use our service, you submit citations for validation. We temporarily process this content to provide our service but do not permanently store it unless necessary for service functionality.

**Contact Information**: If you contact us at support@citationformatchecker.com, we collect your email address and any information you choose to provide in your communication.

**Payment Information**: When you purchase credits, payment information is processed through our third-party payment processor (Stripe). We do not store your credit card information on our servers. We receive only transaction confirmations and payment status.

### Information Automatically Collected

**Analytics Data**: We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you interact with our service, including:
- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Device type, browser type, and operating system
- General geographic location (country/city level)
- Referring websites and search terms
- Click patterns and navigation paths

**Usage Data**: We collect information about your use of our service features, including:
- Number of citations validated
- Types of citation formats checked
- Feature usage patterns
- Error rates and validation results

**Technical Data**: We automatically collect certain technical information, including:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device identifiers
- Session duration and timestamps

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and store certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device.

**Types of Cookies We Use**:
- **Essential Cookies**: Required for the service to function properly, including session management and credit tracking
- **Analytics Cookies**: Help us understand how visitors use our service (Google Analytics)
- **Preference Cookies**: Remember your settings and preferences

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some features of our service.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

**To Provide Our Service**:
- Process and validate your citations
- Manage your credit balance and usage
- Deliver validation results and feedback
- Provide customer support

**To Improve Our Service**:
- Analyze usage patterns and user behavior
- Identify and fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features and improvements
- Optimize user experience and service performance

**To Communicate With You**:
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Send transactional emails (purchase confirmations, credit notifications)
- Provide important service updates and announcements

**For Analytics and Research**:
- Understand how users interact with our service
- Measure the effectiveness of our features
- Conduct research to improve citation validation accuracy

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect, process, or store your information:

**Language Model Providers (OpenAI, Anthropic)**: Your citation content is sent to AI language model providers for validation. These providers process your content according to their own privacy policies and terms of service. We use enterprise API services that do not train models on your data.

**Google Analytics**: We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze usage data. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your use of our service. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

**Stripe**: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. Stripe collects and processes payment information according to its privacy policy. We receive only transaction confirmations and do not store credit card details.

**Hosting and Infrastructure**: Our service is hosted on third-party servers. These providers have access to technical and usage data necessary to provide hosting services.

## Data Retention

**Citation Content**: Citation text submitted for validation is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers. It is temporarily held in memory during processing and then discarded.

**Analytics Data**: Analytics and usage data is retained for up to 26 months to help us understand long-term trends and improve our service.

**Payment Records**: Transaction records are retained for accounting and tax purposes for up to 7 years, as required by law.

**Communication Records**: Support emails and correspondence are retained for up to 2 years to maintain service quality and handle follow-up requests.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including:

- Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS
- Secure server infrastructure with regular security updates
- Access controls and authentication mechanisms
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Secure API communication with third-party services

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
